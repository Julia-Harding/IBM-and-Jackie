Who is IBM

IBM stands for International Business Machines Corporation. It is an American multinational technology corporation with headquarters in New York ane operations in almost 200 countries. The company began in 1911, founded in Endicott, New York and took on its name, "IBM" in 1924.

The Pursuit for New Ideas

IBM is interested in all types of individuals will various pursuits and passions outside of work. They understand that when people explore their interests, it develops a new outlook on the world and enables a broader, more extensive pursuit for new ideas in technology.
